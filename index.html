Penyiraman Sawah

About

SiDrone adalah sebuah usaha teknologi yang mengusung misi untuk meningkatkan efisiensi dan produktivitas industri pertanian.

  • March 2023

    Our Humble Beginnings

    SiDrone bermula dari ide yang muncul dari keprihatinan terhadap para petani yang masih menggunakan cara konvensional dalam menyemprot dan memupuk lahan pertanian mereka. Dalam hal ini, SiDrone memiliki tujuan mulia untuk membantu meningkatkan kesejahteraan para petani dan memajukan industri pertanian secara berkelanjutan.

  • March 2023

    An Business is Born

    SiDrone terlahir dari keikutsertaan dalam Program Pembinaan Mahasiswa Wirausaha yang dibimbing oleh Pak Filmada. Dengan pengalaman dan bimbingan yang diperoleh dari program tersebut, tim SiDrone berhasil menciptakan solusi inovatif menggunakan teknologi drone untuk membantu petani dalam penyiraman sawah, penyiraman pupuk, dan penyiraman pestisida.

DJI Agras T10

The Ideal Drone for New Farmers.

The DJI Agras T10 brings a highly compact-yet-powerful aerial solution to agriculture sites of all sizes and needs. An 8 liter tank and spray width of up to 5 meters allow the aircraft to cover up to 15 acres/hour. Its folding truss structure is sturdy and reliable, enabling efficient folding and unfolding, convenient transportation, and easy transitions.

  • Operation tank volume: 8L
  • High-precision radar: Spherical Omnidirectional Radar System

DJI Agras T20

Intelligent and Powerful.

With its powerful performance and outstanding spraying capabilities, the T20 can conduct autonomous operations over a variety of terrains, such as broadacre farmlands, terraces, and orchards. New features, like an omnidirectional digital radar, have helped the T20 take stability and flight safety to the next level, providing users with highly efficient results.

  • Operation tank volume: 20L
  • Obstacle Avoidance System: Spherical Radar System

DJI Agras T30

A New Flagship for Digital Agriculture.

With a 30 liter spraying tank, the DJI Agras T30 takes aerial spraying efficiency to new heights. A revolutionary transforming body enables more effective spraying, especially for fruit trees. Using DJI digital agriculture solutions, the T30 helps reduce fertilizer use and increase yield with effective, data-driven best practices.

  • Operation tank volume: 30L
  • Obstacle Avoidance System: Omnidirectional Digital Radar
